Graphic Text

GraphicText component has the ability to convert and display any text as an image. Following examples demonstrate generated images with various different font settings.

Source

<h:panelGrid columns="2">
	<h:outputText value="Verdana - 14 - italic : " />
	<p:graphicText value="Visca el Barca!" fontSize="14" fontStyle="italic" />	
	
	<h:outputText value="Arial -  20 - plain : " />
	<p:graphicText value="PrimeFaces is in made in Turkey" fontName="Arial" fontSize="20" />	
	
	<h:outputText value="Tahoma -  12 - plain : " />
	<p:graphicText value="Ultimate Component Suite" fontName="Tahoma" />
	
	<h:outputText value="Georgia -  12 - bold  : " />
	<p:graphicText value="F.C. Barcelona" fontName="Georgia" fontStyle="bold" />		

	<h:outputText value="Courier New  -  18  -  bold : " />
	<p:graphicText value="JSF Rocks!" fontName="Courier New" fontStyle="bold" fontSize="18"/>
</h:panelGrid>
